This web page is dedicated to the fascinating world of cats (Felis catus), small carnivorous mammals that have been cherished companions to humans for thousands of years. Known for their agility, sharp retractable claws, and extraordinary senses. cats are expert hunters with exceptional night vision and acute hearing. Despite their independent nature, they often form deep bonds with humans, expressing affection through purring, head-butting, and playful interactions. Cats are meticulous groomers, using their rough tongues to keep their coats clean and healthy. As obligate carnivores, they require a diet rich in animal-based nutrients such as taurine. Their communication includes a mix of meows, purrs, body language, and scent marking. With a wide range of breeds—such as the elegant Persian, the vocal Siamese, and the sociable Maine Coon—cats offer a variety of looks and temperaments, making them one of the most diverse and popular pets in the world.
